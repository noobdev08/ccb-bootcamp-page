Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this boot camp for?
Code & Create Bootcamp is designed for young people who are curious about tech and eager to learn real, practical skills. It’s perfect for beginners with little or no prior experience, students exploring tech careers, or anyone who wants structured guidance, mentorship, and a supportive learning community to kickstart their journey into technology.
Do I need any prior tech experience to join?
No, you don’t. Code & Create Bootcamp is beginner-friendly and designed for learners with little or no prior tech background. We start from the basics, guide you step-by-step, and provide mentorship, practical exercises, and support to help you grow confidently throughout the program.
How long does the boot camp last and what is the learning format?
The boot camp is fully virtual (on Telegram) and runs for twelve weeks in a structured, cohort-based format. Participants learn through instructor-led sessions, hands-on practical classes, guided mentorship, assignments, and real-world projects. The learning experience is interactive, beginner-friendly, and designed to help you build confidence and practical skills as you progress.
What tracks are available in the boot camp?
Code & Create Bootcamp offers multiple learning tracks to help learners explore different areas of technology. Our core tracks currently include:
  • Web Development
  • Product Design (UI/UX)
  • Product Management
  • Data Analysis
  • 3D Animation
Each track is beginner-friendly, practical, and guided by experienced mentors, ensuring participants gain real skills and hands-on experience. Tracks may expand in future cohorts as the bootcamp grows.
Is the learning practical, and will I work on real projects?
Absolutely. At Code & Create Boot camp, learning is hands-on and fully practical. Participants work on real-world projects throughout the program, including capstone projects that allow learners to apply their skills, solve meaningful problems, and build a portfolio they can showcase to employers. Every project is designed to mirror industry standards and provide authentic, career-ready experience.
How do I join the bootcamp?
Code & Create Boot camp is completely free to join. To become a participant, simply select your preferred track and complete the online application form. Once your application is submitted, the team will review it and guide you through the next steps to join the cohort.
